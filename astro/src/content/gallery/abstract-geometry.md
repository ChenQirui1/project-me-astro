## Finding Art in Architecture

This square composition focuses on the geometric patterns created by modern architecture. The interplay of light and shadow creates an almost abstract quality.

### Compositional Approach

The 1:1 square format works perfectly for this subject, creating a balanced, centered composition that emphasizes the geometric nature of the architecture.

**Design Elements:**
- Strong diagonal lines creating dynamic tension
- High contrast between light and shadow
- Minimalist color palette
- Symmetrical composition

Square format photography challenges you to think differently about composition and balance.
