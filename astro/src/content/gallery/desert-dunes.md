## Desert Solitude

The desert at sunset is a photographer's dream. The warm light creates incredible shadows and highlights on the sand dunes, revealing their sculptural forms.

### Light and Shadow

The interplay between light and shadow on the dunes creates a three-dimensional quality that brings the landscape to life.

**Visual Elements:**
- Warm golden tones from the setting sun
- Strong shadows defining the dune shapes
- Layered composition with multiple dunes
- Clear, dramatic sky

The 4:3 landscape format captures the expansive nature of the desert while maintaining focus on the dune formations.
