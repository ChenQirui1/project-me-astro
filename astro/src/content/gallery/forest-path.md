## The Path Less Traveled

This portrait-oriented photograph captures a forest trail disappearing into the mist. The vertical format emphasizes the depth and draws the eye into the scene.

### Creating Depth

The winding path creates a natural leading line that guides the viewer's eye through the composition. The mist adds mystery and atmosphere.

**Atmospheric Elements:**
- Soft, diffused light filtering through trees
- Mist creating layers of depth
- Natural leading lines from the path
- Rich green tones throughout

The 3:4 portrait format is perfect for emphasizing the journey into the forest depths.
