## About This Shot

This photograph was taken from the rooftop of a downtown building during the magical golden hour. The warm light creates a beautiful contrast with the cool tones of the glass and steel architecture.

### Technical Details

- **Camera**: Canon EOS R5
- **Lens**: 24-70mm f/2.8
- **Settings**: f/8, 1/250s, ISO 400
- **Location**: Downtown Financial District

The composition emphasizes the vertical lines of the buildings while the golden light adds warmth and depth to the scene.
