## Project Overview

This urban photography series explores the intersection of human activity and architectural spaces in modern cities. The project spans multiple locations and captures the raw, unfiltered moments that define urban existence.

![City street scene](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1477959858617-67f85cf4f1df?w=800&h=600&fit=crop)

## Concept & Inspiration

The inspiration came from observing how people interact with their urban environments. Each photograph tells a story of movement, stillness, and the constant rhythm of city life.

### Key Themes

- **Human Connection**: Capturing moments of interaction in public spaces
- **Architectural Geometry**: The interplay of lines, shapes, and shadows
- **Urban Solitude**: Finding quiet moments in bustling environments

![Urban architecture](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1480714378408-67cf0d13bc1b?w=800&h=600&fit=crop)

## Technical Approach

Shot primarily during golden hour and blue hour to capture the most dramatic lighting conditions. Used a combination of wide-angle and telephoto lenses to create varied perspectives.

![Evening cityscape](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1514565131-fce0801e5785?w=800&h=600&fit=crop)

## Exhibition & Reception

The series was exhibited at the Downtown Gallery in Spring 2024, receiving positive feedback from both critics and visitors. Several pieces were acquired for private collections.
